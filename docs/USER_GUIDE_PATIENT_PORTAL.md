## Activating Your Account

### Step 1: Receive Your Invitation

After your healthcare provider creates your patient record, you'll receive an email invitation:

**Email Subject**: "Welcome to ClinicaVoice - Activate Your Patient Portal Account"

**Email Contains**:
- Welcome message
- "Activate Account" button
- Activation link (valid for 7 days)
- Information about portal features

### Step 2: Click the Activation Link

1. Open the invitation email
2. Click the "Activate Account" button
3. You'll be redirected to the activation page

**Important**: The activation link expires after 7 days. If expired, contact your healthcare provider to resend the invitation.

### Step 3: Set Your Password

On the activation page:

1. **Enter Password**
   - Minimum 8 characters
   - Must include:
     - At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
     - At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
     - At least one number (0-9)
     - At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

2. **Confirm Password**
   - Re-enter the same password
   - Must match exactly

3. **Password Strength Indicator**
   - Red: Weak password
   - Yellow: Medium password
   - Green: Strong password

### Step 4: Accept Terms

- Read the Terms and Conditions
- Check the "I accept" box
- This confirms your consent to use the portal

### Step 5: Complete Activation

1. Click "Activate Account"
2. System creates your account
3. Success message appears
4. You're redirected to the login page

### Activation Tips

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Use a strong, unique password
- Save your password securely
- Activate within 7 days
- Check spam folder if email not received

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Share your activation link
- Use simple passwords (like "password123")
- Ignore password requirements
- Wait too long to activate

## Logging In

### First Time Login

1. **Go to Login Page**
   - Use the link provided by your healthcare provider
   - Or navigate to the ClinicaVoice website

2. **Enter Credentials**
   - **Email**: The email address your provider has on file
   - **Password**: The password you set during activation

3. **Click "Sign In"**
   - You'll be redirected to your patient dashboard

### Subsequent Logins

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

### Remember Me Option

- Check "Remember Me" to stay logged in
- Only use on personal devices
- Not recommended on shared computers

### Forgot Password?

1. Click "Forgot Password?" on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Click link and set new password
5. Log in with new password

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Check These First**:
1. ‚úÖ Email address is correct
2. ‚úÖ Password is correct (case-sensitive)
3. ‚úÖ Caps Lock is off
4. ‚úÖ Account has been activated

**Solutions**:
- Use "Forgot Password" to reset
- Check spam folder for activation email
- Contact your healthcare provider
- Clear browser cache and cookies

### Didn't Receive Activation Email

**Check**:
1. Spam/junk folder
2. Email address is correct
3. Email wasn't blocked by filters

**Solutions**:
- Ask provider to resend invitation
- Check with IT department about email filters
- Try different email address

### Activation Link Expired

**What Happened**:
- Links expire after 7 days for security

**Solution**:
- Contact your healthcare provider
- Request new invitation email
- New link will be sent

### Can't See Appointments

**Possible Reasons**:
1. No appointments scheduled
2. Appointments not yet synced
3. Technical issue

**Solutions**:
- Refresh the page
- Log out and log back in
- Contact your provider to verify appointments
- Check back later

### Can't Access Medical Records

**Possible Reasons**:
1. No records available yet
2. Records not yet uploaded
3. Permission issue

**Solutions**:
- Check after your appointment
- Allow time for processing
- Contact your provider

### Page Not Loading

**Try These Steps**:
1. Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try different browser
4. Check internet connection
5. Try on different device

### Mobile Issues

**Common Solutions**:
- Update your browser app
- Clear app cache
- Try desktop version
- Use different browser app

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is the portal available 24/7?**
A: Yes, you can access the portal anytime. However, responses to messages may take 1-2 business days.

**Q: Can I use the portal on my phone?**
A: Yes, the portal is mobile-friendly and works on smartphones and tablets.

**Q: How often is information updated?**
A: Appointment information updates immediately. Medical records are typically available within 24-48 hours after your visit.

**Q: Can family members access my portal?**
A: No, each patient has their own secure account. Family member access is a future feature.

**Q: What if I change my email address?**
A: Contact your healthcare provider to update your email address in their system.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Contact your healthcare provider to discuss account deactivation options.
